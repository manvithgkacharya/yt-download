Step-by-Step Guide

1. Copy YouTube URL

Go to YouTube and copy the URL of the video you want to download from your browser's address bar.

2. Paste URL

Paste the URL into the input field on our homepage and click "Get Formats".

3. Select Format

Choose your preferred video or audio format from the available options.

4. Download

Wait for the download to complete. The file will be saved to your device's default download location.
